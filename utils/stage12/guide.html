Debugging with print

The output from our print statement provides a key piece of evidence: the program crashes in the fourth iteration of the for loop, when i=3.

This means that the indices i and i + 1 are 3 and 4, respectively. On line 15, we use these as indices into lst, which is equal to [1, 2, 3, 4].

Before moving to the next section, try reasoning about which of these indices is the problematic one. Why is it problematic? What can we do to fix it?
