Anatomy of an error message: the Traceback

The previous pair of lines indicated to us that the error occurred during the execution of the sum_pairs function. Moving to the pair of lines above it tells us where the sum_pairs function was called to give us some more context as to the how the error occurred.

  • The first line tells us that the sum_pairs function was called in sum_pairs.py, on line 23, in the main function. Additionally, we see that it's called with the parameter [1, 2, 3, 4].
  • The second line shows us the contents of the line of code in question.
