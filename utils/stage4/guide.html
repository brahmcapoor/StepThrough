Anatomy of an error message: the Traceback

The other important part of an error message--highlighted above-- is the traceback. While this is undeniably the more intimidating-looking of the two sections, it's perhaps your single most powerful tool as you try to isolate the problematic portion of your code.

Fortunately for us, the traceback is particularly well structured and with a firm grasp of that structure, a programmer can quickly isolate what part of their code is causing the exception.
