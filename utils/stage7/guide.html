Anatomy of an error message: the Traceback

The last portion of the Traceback suggested to us that the error in the program's execution occurred during the execution of the main function.

With this in mind, we can move up in the Traceback once more, which gives us two final pieces of information:

  • The first line tells us that the main function was called in sum_pairs.py, on line 27, but not in a function, but rather in the module (sum_pairs.py).
  • The second line shows us the contents of the line of code in question.
